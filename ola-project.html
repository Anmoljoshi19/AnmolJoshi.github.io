Ola Bookings Analysis

About The Project

This project involves generating a synthetic dataset of 100,000 ride-booking records for Bengaluru, analyzing booking trends, cancellations, and ride performance using SQL and Power BI. It provides insights into ride volume, revenue, customer behavior, and rating distributions to support data-driven decision-making.

Key Insights Extracted

  • Generated 96,170 total bookings, resulting in a total booking value of ₹32 Million during the selected period.
  • Approximately 69% of bookings were successfully completed, while the overall cancellation rate stood at 28%, highlighting an opportunity to improve ride completion.
  • Ride demand remained consistent throughout February, with a slight increase in booking volume toward the end of the month.
  • Bike (₹6.90M) and E-Bike (₹6.91M) generated the highest booking value among all vehicle categories.
  • Prime SUV achieved the highest successful booking revenue (₹4.83M), indicating strong customer preference for premium rides.
  • The average ride distance remained close to 11 km across all vehicle types, reflecting consistent travel patterns.
  • Driver-side cancellations accounted for the largest share of revenue loss, making them the primary contributor to unsuccessful rides.
  • The most common customer cancellation reasons included AC issues, incorrect pickup address, driver delays, and changes in travel plans, while driver cancellations were mainly due to personal/vehicle-related and customer-related issues.
  • Driver and customer ratings remained consistently around 4.0/5 across all vehicle categories, indicating a generally positive user experience and service quality.
  • The analysis highlights opportunities to reduce ride cancellations, improve driver availability, and enhance operational efficiency to increase successful bookings and overall revenue.
