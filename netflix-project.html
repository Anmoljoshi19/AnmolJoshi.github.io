Netflix Content Strategy

About The Project

This project analyzes Netflix's content to uncover trends in movies, TV shows, genres, and ratings. The dataset was cleaned and structured in MySQL for smooth analysis. Key focus areas include content distribution, top-rated shows, country-wise contributions, and recent additions.

Key Insights Extracted

  • Actor and director trend analysis identified the most frequently featured talent, helping understand recurring collaborations and content patterns.
  • Genre distribution analysis revealed the most popular content categories, providing insights into viewer preferences and content strategy.
  • The Top 5 countries contributed the majority of Netflix's content, demonstrating the platform's strong presence in key global markets.
  • 2020 recorded the highest number of movie releases, reflecting a surge in content production and expansion during that period.
  • Movies dominate Netflix's content library, significantly outnumbering TV shows, highlighting the platform's strong focus on feature-length entertainment.
