MySQL & PowerBI

Myntra Fashion Trend Analysis

About The Project

This project contains comprehensive SQL scripts for analyzing a massive dataset of Myntra's fashion clothing items, focusing on extracting actionable insights for business growth and retail trend forecasting.

Key Insights Extracted

  • Indian Wear was the top-performing category, followed by Bottom Wear and Topwear, indicating strong customer demand for ethnic and casual fashion.
  • Puma emerged as the highest-performing brand, with HERE&NOW and HRX also contributing significantly to sales.
  • The platform featured 199,999 products with an average customer rating of 3.9/5, reflecting overall positive customer satisfaction.
  • Products offered discounts of up to 90%, demonstrating the importance of promotional pricing in driving customer purchases.
  • Gender-wise analysis revealed varying preferences across product categories, providing valuable insights for targeted marketing, inventory planning, and personalized recommendations.
